Aerial Robotics Kharagpur
ARK

We are a student-run research group building autonomous aerial vehicles, based at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.

OVERVIEW

Autonomous Flying Vehicles

We aim to move the state-of-the-art in aerial robotics forward. Our quadrotors, when ready, would be capable of autonomous flight, localization in GPS denied environments, tracking and interacting with mobile ground robots as well as other drones, and much more!

International Aerial Robotics Competition

IARC is the longest running collegiate aerial robotics challenge in the world. Entering its third decade, the competition continues to tackle challenges that are currently impossible for any flying robots owned by government or industry. We aim to participate in its next iteration.
